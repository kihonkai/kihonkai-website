http://jisho.org/kanji/details/%E5%88%86

Other meanings

分 - ぶん - amount; quantity

I'm not going to add compounds for this Kanji. The entry in the Kanji Learner's Dictionary was far longer than any others I've come across so far. At a later date I'll go over other meanings. For now its use as counter for minutes is the main one I've come across.
