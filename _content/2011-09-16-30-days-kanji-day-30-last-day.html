買う - かう - buy
http://jisho.org/kanji/details/%E8%B2%B7

Some Compounds

買い手 - かいて - Buyer, customer
買い物 - かいもの - Shopping

This has been quite a simple Kanji to finish on, but one of the few that I felt I couldn't just put the Kanji on it's own in the first entry. So that includes the dictionary form. The original form I had learnt was the polite form 買います.

This ends the series now. It's been an interesting challenge, I'm going to post a recap over the weekend and talk a bit more about my experience with it.
